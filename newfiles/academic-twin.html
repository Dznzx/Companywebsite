FAQ

Questions answered.

What file types can I upload?+
Currently we support PDF files, with support for Word documents, PowerPoint slides, images (via OCR), and plain text coming soon. You can upload multiple files per subject and organize them into folders.
Is my data private and secure?+
Yes. Every user gets their own isolated namespace in our vector database. Your documents are never shared with other users or used to train our AI models. We use Supabase Row Level Security and Clerk authentication for enterprise-grade security.
How is this different from ChatGPT?+
ChatGPT answers from generic training data. Academic Twin answers from your specific uploaded materials using Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). Every answer is grounded in your own notes and cites exactly which page and document it came from.
When will Academic Twin launch?+
Academic Twin is live right now! Go to academic-twin-rouge.vercel.app to sign up for free. First 3 months are completely free, then ₹50/month.
Is there a student discount?+
Yes — we're building this for students, so our Pro plan at ₹299/month (~$3.50 USD) already reflects student-friendly global pricing. We'll also offer institutional bulk pricing for universities and coaching centres.
Can my university or school use Academic Twin?+
Absolutely. We're building B2B institutional plans with teacher dashboards, class analytics, and bulk student licensing. Email us at hello@veltrongroup.ai to discuss a partnership.
