FAQ

Questions answered.

Is this a replacement for my doctor?+
No. Veltron Health AI provides general wellness guidance and helps you understand symptoms, but it's never a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. We always recommend seeing a doctor for serious or persistent symptoms.
How is my health data protected?+
All health data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We never sell or share your personal health information with third parties. You can delete your data at any time.
What languages will be supported?+
We're launching with English and Tamil, with Hindi and other major Indian languages planned shortly after, expanding globally based on demand.
When does it launch?+
Veltron Health AI is in active development as part of our Phase 3 roadmap. Join the waitlist to be notified the moment early access opens.
