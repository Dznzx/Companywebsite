FAQ

Questions answered.

What tools does WorkOS integrate with?+
At launch, WorkOS integrates with Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, Google Workspace, and Slack. More integrations including Notion, Asana, and Jira are planned shortly after.
Is my company's data secure?+
Yes. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit, with per-organization data isolation. We never use your company's data to train shared AI models.
How is this different from other AI meeting tools?+
WorkOS isn't just a meeting notetaker — it's a full workplace AI that connects meetings, tasks, and documents into one continuous workflow, priced specifically for SMEs who can't afford enterprise suites.
When does it launch?+
Veltron WorkOS is part of our Phase 3 roadmap. Join the waitlist and you'll be among the first to get beta access.
