FAQ

Questions answered.

Is this licensed financial advice?+
No. Veltron FinAI provides general financial education and guidance, not licensed financial, tax, or investment advice. For complex decisions, we recommend consulting a licensed financial advisor.
Is my financial data safe?+
Yes. We use bank-grade encryption and never store your banking credentials directly. Account connections use secure, read-only protocols similar to those used by major fintech apps.
Which markets will FinAI support?+
We're launching with India-focused features (INR, Indian investment products, tax rules) with expansion to Southeast Asia and other emerging markets planned next.
When does it launch?+
Veltron FinAI is part of our Phase 4 roadmap, planned for 2027 onward. Join the waitlist to be notified as development progresses.
